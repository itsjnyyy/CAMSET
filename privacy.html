📷 CAMSET Bot — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CAMSET ("the Bot") handles information when you use it in a Discord server. We are committed to protecting your privacy — and the short version is: we collect nothing.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CAMSET does not collect, store, log, or share any of the following:

  • Discord usernames, user IDs, or profile information
  • Message content (beyond what is needed to process a command in real time)
  • Server names, server IDs, or membership information
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Any personally identifiable information

2. How Commands Are Processed

When you run /camset <player>, the Bot receives the player name you provided, fetches data from Liquipedia's public website, and returns a response directly in Discord. No part of this interaction is stored after the response is sent.

3. Third-Party Services

The Bot retrieves data from Liquipedia, a publicly accessible wiki. Your query is not sent to Liquipedia in any identifiable form — the Bot fetches the full player list and filters it locally. Please refer to Liquipedia's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

The Bot is hosted on Railway. Railway may collect infrastructure-level logs (e.g. server uptime, error traces) as part of their hosting service. These logs do not contain user data. See Railway's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Data Retention

No user data is retained. Each command is handled statelessly — once a response is sent, all associated data is discarded.

5. Children's Privacy

CAMSET does not knowingly interact with or collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has used the Bot in violation of this policy, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
